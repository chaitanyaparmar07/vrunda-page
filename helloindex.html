Vrunda, Tum Bahut Special Ho

❤️

Shayad har koi tumhari muskurahat ki ahmiyat nahi samajh paaye,
lekin mere liye tumhari ek smile duniya ki sabse pyaari cheez hai.

Tumhari baatein, tumhara andaaz, tumhara hona – sab kuch alag hai.
Tum jaise ho, waise hi perfect ho.
Aur mujhe khushi hoti hai tumhein jaankar, tumse baat karke.

Agar kabhi udaas mehsoos karo, toh yaad rakhna –
tumhara ek silent supporter hamesha tumhare saath hai.

With all my warmth and respect,
— Chaitanya
